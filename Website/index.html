About Us

Learn more about Talk to Text AI

Revolutionizing Speech Recognition

Talk to Text AI was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: to make speech recognition technology accessible to everyone. Our team of AI experts, linguists, and software engineers have created a platform that transforms how people interact with technology.

We believe that voice is the most natural form of human communication, and our technology bridges the gap between spoken word and digital text. Whether you're a professional creating documents, a student taking notes, or someone looking to communicate more effectively, our AI-powered solution is designed for you.

With over 1 million users worldwide and recognition from leading tech publications, we're proud to be at the forefront of speech recognition innovation.
